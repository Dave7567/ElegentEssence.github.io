ABOUT US

ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Here at Elegant essence, we are dedicated to providing our customers with high-quality jewellery and exceptional customer service. We have been in business for over 20 years and have a team of experienced professionals who are passionate about what they do. Our mission is to provide our customers with only the very best jewellery at reasonable prices. Established on the 8th of March 2003 by six creative and innovative individuals, our company will always prove to be the superior jewellery supplier.

MISSION

The story of Elegant essence is founded on audacity. Since its creation in Lusaka by Darshit Dave in 2003, Elegant essence has evolved through welcoming visionaries and free spirits into the company. Today this pioneering spirit is more alive than ever. Pushing the boundaries, changing the use or meaning to reveal beauty, setting off on an adventure to distant horizons and embracing the rich diversity of other cultures, this creative energy is fundamental to everything, every day.
